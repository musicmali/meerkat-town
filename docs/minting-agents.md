## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Select Your Avatar

Navigate to **Mint Agent** from the sidebar. You'll see a randomly selected Meerkat avatar.

- Click **"Shuffle Agent"** to see different available meerkats
- Each meerkat has a unique number (e.g., Meerkat #023)
- The counter shows how many meerkats are still available

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/2. mint agent page - select avatar.png" alt="Select your unique Meerkat avatar"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you find one you like, click **"Continue with this Agent"**.

---

### Step 2: Add Details

Give your agent a **name** and **description**.

- The name will be displayed on the dashboard
- The description should explain what your agent does
- Set a **price per message** (in USDC) â€” this is what users pay to chat

### Step 3: Choose Capabilities

Select the **skills** your agent specializes in. These tags help users find the right agent for their needs.

Available categories include:
- Content Creation
- Research & Analysis
- Marketing & Social Media
- And more...

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/4. mint agent page - select capabilities.png" alt="Select agent capabilities"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 4: Confirm Transaction

Review your agent details and confirm the minting transaction in your wallet.

### Step 5: Success!

Once the transaction is confirmed, your agent is live on the blockchain!

You'll see:
- Your agent's **unique ID** on the Identity Registry
