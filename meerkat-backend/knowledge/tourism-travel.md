# Tourism & Travel Fundamentals

## Travel Planning

### Pre-Trip Research
- Destination overview and culture
- Best time to visit (weather, crowds, prices)
- Visa requirements and entry rules
- Vaccination requirements
- Local customs and etiquette
- Safety advisories

### Booking Essentials
- Flights: Compare prices (Google Flights, Skyscanner)
- Accommodation: Hotels, hostels, Airbnb, vacation rentals
- Transportation: Car rental, trains, local transit
- Travel insurance: Medical, cancellation, baggage
- Activities and tours: Pre-book popular attractions

### Budget Planning
- Accommodation (typically 30-40% of budget)
- Transportation (flights, local travel)
- Food and dining
- Activities and attractions
- Emergency fund (10-15% buffer)
- Currency exchange considerations

## Travel Documentation

### Passports
- Check validity (6+ months often required)
- Blank pages for stamps
- Renewal timeline (weeks to months)
- Emergency replacement while abroad

### Visas
- Visa-free destinations
- Visa on arrival
- E-visa applications
- Embassy/consulate applications
- Transit visas

### Other Documents
- Travel insurance documents
- Hotel confirmations
- Flight itineraries
- Vaccination records
- International driving permit
- Copies of important documents

## Accommodation Types

### Hotels
- Rated by stars (1-5)
- Amenities and services
- Loyalty programs
- Best for: Convenience, reliability

### Hostels
- Dormitory or private rooms
- Social atmosphere
- Shared facilities
- Best for: Budget travelers, solo travelers

### Vacation Rentals
- Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com
- Full apartments or houses
- Kitchen facilities
- Best for: Families, longer stays

### Boutique Hotels
- Unique character and design
- Personalized service
- Local flavor
- Best for: Experience seekers

### Resorts
- All-inclusive options
- On-site activities
- Multiple dining options
- Best for: Relaxation, families

## Safety and Health

### Travel Safety
- Research local safety concerns
- Register with embassy
- Share itinerary with family
- Avoid displaying valuables
- Use hotel safes
- Be aware of common scams

### Health Precautions
- Required/recommended vaccinations
- Prescription medications
- Travel health kit
- Food and water safety
- Altitude sickness awareness
- Jet lag management

### Travel Insurance
- Medical coverage abroad
- Trip cancellation/interruption
- Baggage loss/delay
- Emergency evacuation
- 24/7 assistance hotlines

- Reduce plastic use

### Responsible Tourism
